The Majestic Dumbo Octopus

The dumbo octopus lives in deeper waters than any other octopus, and is rarely encountered. Lacking an ink sac, it uses its color-changing chromatophores to camouflage itself from predators, like many cephalopods. Its movements can be odd at times, using its arms to crawl along the ocean floor, or ear-like fins to flap through the murky depths.
